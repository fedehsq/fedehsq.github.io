Privacy Policy - FollowGuard

Last update: August 2025

1. Data Collection

The App does not collect personal data such as name, email, or contact details. Only technical data necessary for security, crash reporting, and subscription management is processed.

2. Services Used

  • freeRASP by Talsec - security checks against fraud, root/jailbreak detection, and app integrity. Some diagnostic data may be sent to Talsec.
  • Firebase (Core, App Check, Crashlytics) - used for app integrity verification, crash reports, and protection against abuse.
  • In-App Purchases (Google Play / App Store) - used to manage subscriptions. These services may process your account data according to Google/Apple policies.

3. Purpose of Data Use

  • Ensure the security and integrity of the App.
  • Detect fraud and prevent unauthorized use.
  • Manage subscription status.
  • Improve stability and fix crashes.

4. Data Sharing

Technical data may be shared with the above providers (Google, Apple, Talsec) for the purposes described. No personal data is sold or shared with other third parties.

5. User Rights

User Rights Since the App does not collect or store personal data, no action from the user is required. Any account or subscription data is handled directly by Google or Apple according to their policies.
